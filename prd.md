# Product Requirements Document (PRD): 1Life Network Website

## 1. Project Overview
**1Life Network** is a culture and talent engine building globally competitive African creative output through systems, standards, and execution. Based in Accra with a global vision, it discovers, develops, produces, distributes, and monetizes talent.

**Tagline:** *Talent Needs A System.*

## 2. Primary Objectives
The website serves as the digital headquarters for 1Life Network, designed to **Define the Brand** and **Convert Visitors**.
*   **Explain 1Life:** Communicate the ecosystem and its value proposition.
*   **Build Credibility:** Establish 1Life as a serious media and culture brand.
*   **Attract Sponsors:** Show professional infrastructure for brand partnerships.
*   **Collect Talent Submissions:** Streamline the pipeline for artist discovery.
*   **Sell Services:** Monetize production and creative capabilities.
*   **Showcase Highlights:** Feature flagship content (1Life Sessions).

## 3. Visual & Creative Direction
*   **Aesthetic:** Dark cinematic, premium, and professional.
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   **Primary:** Black (dominant)
    *   **Secondary:** White (text)
    *   **Accent:** Red (careful and intentional use)
*   **Typography:** Strong, clean hierarchy with modern typefaces.
*   **Experience:** Smooth modern animations, mobile-first responsive design, and fast loading speeds.

## 4. Site Structure & Content Sections
1.  **Hero:** Strong immediate impact, premium feel, clear value statement.
2.  **About 1Life:** Narrative-driven storytelling explaining who, what, why, and where.
3.  **What We Do / Ecosystem:** Detail the discovery-to-monetization pipeline.
4.  **1Life Sessions (Flagship):** 
    *   **Concept & Value:** Position as the flagship platform under 1Life Network. Introduce the concept and explain the inherent value.
    *   **Live Formats:** Showcase the diverse formats produced:
        *   Live Performances
        *   DJ Live Sessions
        *   Acoustic Sessions
        *   Band Sessions
        *   Freestyle / Cypher Sessions
        *   Special Collaborations
    *   **Aesthetic & Tone:** This section should feel premium, exciting, and culturally important.
    *   **Direct CTAs:** Watch on YouTube, Explore Sessions, Subscribe Now.
    *   **Note:** The website explains the platform and its value; YouTube remains the primary host for the content.

5.  **Submit Talent:** Professional submission form for artists and creatives.
6.  **Services:** Clear list of professional services offered by the network.
7.  **Partners / Sponsors:** Dedicated section for credibility and inquiry.
8.  **Contact:** Standard professional inquiry channel.

## 5. Functional Requirements
*   **Artist Submission Form:** Integrated form for talent intake.
*   **Backend Editing:** Easy content updates for the client.
*   **Social Media Integration:** Seamless links to YouTube, Instagram, etc.
*   **SEO Optimization:** SEO-ready structure and meta-tags.
*   **Performance:** Optimized for speed and mobile responsiveness.

## 6. Technical Stack (Proposed)
*   **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Vanilla), JavaScript.
*   **Animations:** CSS transitions/animations or lightweight JS libraries (e.g., GSAP).
*   **Hosting/Backend:** To be determined (needs to support easy content editing).

## 7. Success Metrics
*   Increased talent submissions.
*   High engagement with YouTube showcase links.
*   Professional perception from potential sponsors and partners.
